Privacy Policy for PhoneLum

       Effective Date: June 1, 2026

       At Shenzhen Yinshu Electronics Co., Ltd, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard any information that you provide when using our "PhoneLum" app. Our app is a standalone application designed to connect via Bluetooth to hardware devices without requiring user registration or login. It does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

       Our app does not collect any personal information from users. The only data it handles is related to the functionality of connecting to and controlling hardware devices via Bluetooth. No personal data is stored on our servers or transmitted to third parties.

2. Use of Collected Information

       Since no personal information is collected, there is no use of such information. All interactions within the app are confined to the device itself and do not involve any external entities or services.

3. Sharing of Information

       We do not share any information because no personal information is collected by our app. There are no third-party dependencies or integrations that would require sharing of data.

4. Data Security

       Although no personal information is collected, we take reasonable steps to ensure the security of the Bluetooth connection between the app and the connected hardware devices. These measures include implementing industry-standard encryption protocols to protect against unauthorized access.

5. Children's Privacy

       Our app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently received any information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

6. Your Rights

       As our app does not collect any personal information, it does not affect any specific rights under applicable privacy laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, if you have any concerns about your privacy while using our app, please feel free to reach out to us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

       We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
